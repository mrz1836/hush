SIGINT/SIGTERM during approval wait (FR-021): `signal.NotifyContext` cancel propagates through defer chain → key zeroed → no client-initiated `/claim/cancel` (server-side TTL handles cleanup). `--machine-index` is required on every invocation (no client config file, no env-var fallback per FR-020 pattern).
